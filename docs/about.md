## Research Context

The repository implements the statutory rules of the Swedish tax-and-transfer system and
provides a behavioural microsimulator for evaluating tax progressivity and policy
counterfactuals. It covers every major reform episode since the 1991 tax reform, with a
versioned legislative-parameter ledger validated against public statutes (SFS).
