## Interoperable Europe Portal

A submission to the Interoperable Europe Portal (https://interoperable-europe.ec.europa.eu)
is planned within 30 days of the first stable release, as required by the Interoperable
Europe Act (EU) 2024/903 for publicly funded research software.
